# ChroniNotes Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ChroniNotes ("the app", "we", "our", "us") handles information when you use the ChroniNotes desktop application.

ChroniNotes is designed to be offline-first. Your notes, tasks, calendar events, timer data, and preferences are stored locally on your device.

## 1. Information We Process

### 1.1 Data you create in the app
ChroniNotes processes the content you add, including:
- Notes and folders
- Tasks and task status
- Calendar events and reminders
- Focus/timer sessions and presets
- Profile and app preferences (for example: name, avatar, theme)

This data is stored locally on your device.

### 1.2 Technical data from optional online features
ChroniNotes has limited online features:
- Update checks and update downloads from GitHub Releases
- Font loading from Google Fonts

When these features are used, third-party services may receive technical data such as your IP address, request headers, device/network metadata, and timestamp, as part of standard web requests.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information only to:
- Provide core app functionality
- Save your data locally and restore it when you reopen the app
- Show desktop reminders you configure
- Check for and download app updates when requested
- Render app fonts

We do not use your app content for advertising.

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not transmit your notes, tasks, calendar content, or timer logs to our servers.

Limited data may be processed by third-party providers when online features are used:
- GitHub (for release metadata and installer downloads)
- Google Fonts (for font delivery)

Those providers process data according to their own privacy policies:
- GitHub Privacy Statement: https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 4. Data Storage and Retention

ChroniNotes stores data locally, including:
- SQLite database files
- Local JSON files
- Local app settings (for example, WebView local storage)

Retention is controlled by you. Data remains on your device until you delete it from the app or remove app data manually.

## 5. Security

We use reasonable technical measures appropriate for a local-first desktop app. No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

You are responsible for protecting access to your device and user account on the operating system.

## 6. Your Choices and Controls

You can:
- Use the app offline for core features
- Disable network access at the OS/network level
- Remove your data by deleting app data files
- Uninstall the app at any time

You can access your information directly:
- Inside the app UI (notes, tasks, events, sessions, and settings)
- Through local files stored on your device

Windows app data path is typically:
`C:\Users\<your-user>\AppData\Roaming\com.chroninotes.app\`

## 7. Children's Privacy

ChroniNotes is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children through our own servers.

## 8. International Use

If you use optional online features, third-party providers may process technical request data in countries outside your location.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective date" above shows the latest revision date.
